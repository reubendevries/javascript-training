    | --- | --- | --- | ---
    | Chrome | <kbd>Ctrl + Alt + J</kbd> | <kbd>Cmd + Opt + J</kbd> | <kbd>Ctrl + Alt + J</kbd>
    | Firefox | <kbd>Ctrl + Alt + I</kbd> | <kbd>Cmd + Opt + I</kbd> | <kbd>Ctrl + Alt + I</kbd>
    | Safari | N/A | <kbd>Cmd + Opt + C</kbd> | N/A
    | Opera | <kbd>Ctrl + Shift + I</kbd> | <kbd>Cmd + Opt + I </kbd> | <kbd>Ctrl + Shift + I</kbd> 
    | Edge | <kbd>Ctrl + Shift + I</kbd> | <kbd>Cmd + Opt + I </kbd> | <kbd>Ctrl + Shift + I</kbd>
